Twin Nations

UX/UI design / App design / Branding / Advertising

The problem

86% of young travellers say that experiencing new cultures is their reason for travelling.

However, it is often hard to gain an authentic experience of local cultures as traditional travel guides typically only cover topics like tourist attractions and restaurants, but with little attention paid to the everyday lives of local people.

How can we help young travellers fully immerse themselves in a new culture?

The solution

My solution is an online travel guide that compares the lifestyles of a traveller’s home country and their destination country side by side.

Because there is such a wide variety of countries and cultures throughout the world, I have narrowed this down to Chinese tourists travelling to Italy as an example to answer this question.

Prototype

The direction of Twin Nations’ Chinese text is inspired by traditional Chinese writing which goes from top to bottom and from right to left, contrasting with its Italian counterpart.

The app is fairly simple to use since it has only one main feature, which is the comparison section. Split screen is used to display the home country at the top and the destination country at the bottom.

 

This example is designed for a specific situation where a Chinese tourist travel to Italy. By using this formula as a guide, the differences between any two cultures can be compared easily by putting them side by side. Whether it’s Brits travelling to Malaysia or Greeks travelling to Egypt, the possibilities are endless.
